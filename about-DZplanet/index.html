About DZplanet.

We are here to help you through your journey in Algeria.

Our goal is to promote tourism in Algeria by suggesting great destinations and common information about the country covering as many cities as possible, all of which will help visitiors with their journey and hopefully make their experience memorable.

Our Story
We are a group of third year NTIC students and part of SCC university club, we believe that anything beneficial for our country is totally worth working and the striving for, we also believe that Algeria is endowed with an unexploited potential that could enable it to be a leading tourist destination, that's in a nutshell how we came up with the idea of DZplanet as a website dedicated to help tourism sector in Algeria grow.

Why tourism?
Tourism is vital for the success of many economies around the world. There are several benefits of tourism on host destinations. Tourism boosts the revenue of the economy, creates thousands of jobs, develops the infrastructures of a country, and plants a sense of cultural exchange between foreigners and citizens, it also helps to preserve heritage sites, natural wonders, and precious cultures, by creating space for people to showcase their cultural traditions and protect sacred areas.

Why Algeria?
Algeria is one of the most interesting countries in North Africa and one of the friendliest. Yet, it remains an unknown destination. Pristine Sahara, gorgeous coastline, beautiful cultural sites, bustling cities, and mouth-watering food, it is also a country with geographical contrasts, with great differences, from the green valleys in between steep mountains in the north to some of the most extensive sand dune regions of all Sahara.Algeria is the place to visit if you want to get off the beaten track.
